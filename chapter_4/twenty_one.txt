Card    Value

2-10    face value
J,Q,K   10
A       1 or 11

Deck 52-cards 4 suits (hearts, diamonds, clubs, spades)
2 Players Dealer and Player
Deal 2 cards
Get as close to 21 as possible without going over

Player Turn
  - hit or stay ?
  - bust if over 21

Dealer turn
  - starts when player says 'Stay'
  - has to hit until total at least 17
  - bust if over 21

Compare
  - Highest value wins not over 21 wins

Overall steps

1. Initialize deck
2. Deal cards to player and dealer
3. Player turn: hit or stay
    - repeat until bust or stay
4. If player bust, dealer wins.
5. Dealer turn: hit or stay
    - repeat until total >= 17
6. If dealer bust, player wins.
7. Compare cards and declare winner.
